About Us

This library website serves as a comprehensive digital gateway for accessing the library's resources and services. It allows users to browse the online catalog, which includes books, ebooks, audiobooks, and other media, and easily check availability or place holds on items. In addition to physical resources, many city libraries provide digital access to ebooks, audiobooks, research databases, and even streaming media, making it convenient to borrow materials from home. The website also offers essential services like account management, where users can renew borrowed items, view due dates, and track their reading history.
