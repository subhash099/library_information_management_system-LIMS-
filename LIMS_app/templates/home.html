why reading is important?

Reading is important because it enhances knowledge, stimulates mental growth, and improves critical thinking skills. It exposes individuals to different perspectives, cultures, and ideas, broadening their understanding of the world. Regular reading strengthens language and communication abilities, enriching vocabulary and improving writing skills. Additionally, it boosts cognitive functions such as concentration, focus, and memory retention.
